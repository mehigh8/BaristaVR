# BaristaVR

Small VR game made for a project of the Introduction to Virtual and Augmented Reality course of the Graphics, Multimedia and Virtual Reality master program.

In the game you are playing as a barista trying to serve curstomers. They can order coffee with different amounts of milk and various food items from the store. You have to complete their orders correctly in order to score points.
